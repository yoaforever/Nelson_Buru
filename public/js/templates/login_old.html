PayAnyBiz Payables

Payables for company large or small is often a very inefficient cost center. With PayAnyBiz, that task is easily managed and completely automated. You now can manage all of your payables with an easy to use cloud base solution at a fraction of the cost you are spending today!
With PayAnyBiz Payables, you can send any business a payment. You can manually input the one off invoice that needs to be paid immediately or through our easy plug-in solution, you can upload a batch of payments that need to be paid without the need to manually create each payment. Your payment and data will be sent to your vendor for easy reconciliation. You can create your vendor or ask them to join the PayAnyBiz platform. The benefits for both you and your vendors can be impactful to both your respective companies.
You can schedule payments on a certain due date, make a payment or approve a group of payments. You can also segregate your payables, i.e. an ocean freight transaction to the ocean division and air freight to the air freight division. An added benefit is the state of the art PayAnyBiz Dispute Resolution Tool. hyper link You can now dispute incorrect invoices directly with your vendor and fully communicate with your vendor via PayAnyBiz Chat. This will increase your communication with your vendor as well as strengthen your relationships. PayAnyBiz allows you to effectuate payments in many different ways, you can log on to your account at www.payanybiz.com , in your own operating system, by email or from your mobile devise. With PayAnyBiz it allows your company to be in complete control of its cash flow all the time.

PayAnyBiz eliminates the following traditional processes for the payor:
  • Reduction of time, personnel, and stress associated with the payment process.
  • Avoid check issuance, required signature(s), messengers or couriers.
  • Immediate recognition of payment reducing or eliminating posting delays.
  • Avoid further costs due such as demurrage, storage, and inspections when payment is late.
  • Avoid having credit placed on hold due to discrepancies.
  • Avoid collection calls, e-mails, and faxes.


The process improvements translate into the following benefits for the Payors:
  • Consolidate all of your payables in one easy to use solution.
  • No upfront cost to implement .
  • Real time accounts payable management tool.
  • Reduction of time, personnel, costs and mistakes associated with the payment process.
  • Ability to have both your payable information and credit source all in one solution. With additional credit terms.
  • Document storage keeping the documents and payment together in the corresponding transaction.
  • No more need to wire transfer, cut checks, mail, overnight packages, email payment details.

PayAnyBiz Dispute Resolution

Today many disputes that are not priorities are swept under the rug and not attended until they become emergencies. The costs associated with reconciling these disputes are completely non-productive. With PayAnyBiz, all the information for each transaction is easily available to both parties, facilitating a rapid, amicable solution. No missed phone calls or emails. Our dispute resolution tool is completely customizable to fit your business. You can now communicate with PayAnyBiz Chat directly with your customer or vendor in real-time. This tool will hold your personnel, your customers or vendor accountable. You now can now be in complete control of disputes at a fraction of the cost. Managers can now communicate with their team internally with PayAnyBiz Chat to follow up to ensure a prompt response to a dispute, and they can also schedule tasks with reminders to make your team more efficient.
